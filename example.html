About Us

EDUSN International School

EDUSN School is a private, international school like no other. We are one of all-digital schools in the world for KG through K twelve (AS & A Level). Our curriculum is tailored to an individualized learning model with a blended digital learning designed specifically for students on their way to college. Our community is approachable and inclusive. We focus on building a real life of character, ready to greet college and beyond. And, despite our rigorous and competitive spirit, our environment is uniquely supportive. We have built EDUSN Digital School that way for a reason__it’s how kids learn best.
